About Me

My name is Brian Arsenault. I spent the first 32 years of my life growing up in Connecticut. I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Marketing from Central Connecticut State University in New Britain, CT. I have worked all types of jobs throughout my life but my most memorable one was with a company called Nutmeg Exhibits as a project manager. This job made me bring out my creativity in helping to design some of the most complex ideas and bring them to life. After 9 years of that, my wife and I decided to pack up and move across the country to AZ. I had a job as a project manager position for a steel fabricating company but left that as my wife and I introduced our first child. I got into coding bootcamp in hopes to turn my creative ideas into a career that will let me showcase my skills to an employer.
