A little bit about me!

After 9 years of that, my wife and I decided to pack up and move across the country to AZ. I had a job as a project manager position for a steel fabricating company but left that as my wife and I introduced our first child. I got into coding bootcamp in hopes to turn my creative ideas into a career that will let me showcase my skills to an employer.
